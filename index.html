Green Plus Nepal (G+ Nepal)

Advancing Climate Resilience through Finance, Nature & Innovation

About Us

G+ Nepal is a pioneering organization dedicated to sustainable development and climate resilience. We bridge environmental challenges with finance-driven strategies, empowering communities, businesses, and governments to transition toward a low-carbon future.

Our Expertise

Climate Finance for Adaptation - Mitigation: Designing bankable projects for accessing climate funds transparently and effectively.
Nature-Based Solutions: Climate-smart agriculture, agroforestry, and ecosystem restoration to enhance biodiversity and livelihoods.
Green Technology Supply Chain: Access to clean energy, waste-to-resource, and energy-efficient technologies for rural/urban resilience.
Eco-Innovation Ecosystem: Supporting R&D, piloting scalable green business models, and startup incubation.
Climate Auditing: Measuring footprints, adaptation progress, compliance (ISO 14064, Gold Standard), and policy knowledge management.
End-to-End Project Support: Full-cycle project support from design to execution, aligned with global climate standards.
